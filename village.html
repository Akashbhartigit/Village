Village Facilities

Education

Education is a lifelong process of acquiring knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes. It involves formal instruction in schools, colleges, or universities, as well as informal learning through experiences.

Healthcare

Healthcare refers to the organized system of medical services and facilities designed to promote, maintain, and restore health. It includes medical professionals, hospitals, clinics, and preventive care measures.

Agriculture

Agriculture is the practice of cultivating the soil, growing crops, and raising livestock for human use. It is a vital industry that provides food, fibers, and raw materials. Agriculture involves various activities such as planting, harvesting, and animal husbandry.

Community Center

A community center is a multifunctional space that serves as a hub for various social, recreational, and educational activities within a community. It provides a gathering place for residents to engage in events, meetings, and cultural programs..
